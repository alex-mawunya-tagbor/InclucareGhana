Meet Our Dedicated Team

At Inclusive Care Foundation, our team is united by a shared commitment to equity, inclusion, and community transformation. Each member brings unique expertise and passion to our mission of advancing inclusive development across Ghana.

Divine Elikplim Wunu

Executive Director

A professional teacher and certified Community-Based Rehabilitation (CBR) Specialist, Divine leads the strategic vision of the Foundation. His dual expertise in education and rehabilitation drives our mission to empower marginalized communities and advocate for inclusive policies nationwide.

Albert Worlanyo Atidakpo

Director of Operations

As a trained CBR Officer, Albert oversees the Foundation’s operational systems and program implementation. His leadership ensures that our projects are community-driven, sustainable, and aligned with our core values of equity and inclusiveness.

Rejoice Gblorkpor

Finance Officer

With a background in banking and Business Administration, Rejoice manages the Foundation’s financial planning, reporting, and compliance. He ensures transparency and accountability in all fiscal operations.

Phidelia Ametame

Director of Fundraising

A certified CBR Officer with a focus on resource mobilization, Phidelia designs and leads our fundraising strategies. She cultivates partnerships and secures support to sustain our inclusive development programs.

Elkanah Sitsofe Dogbey

Project Officer

A professional teacher with a heart for community service, Elkanah coordinates our field projects and community engagements. He ensures that our programs are responsive to local needs and deliver measurable impact.

Pius Kwabena Afrim

P.R.O

A church catechist and trained Human Resource Management professional, Pius leads our communications and public engagement. He builds bridges between the Foundation and the public, amplifying our message and fostering trust.

Rev. Binda Kwesi Danku

Patron

An ordained minister and professional teacher, Rev. Danku serves as the spiritual and moral guide of the Foundation. As Patron, he provides wisdom, encouragement, and oversight to ensure our work remains grounded in compassion and justice.

Cynthia Kportorgbi

Patron

A Pediatric Nurse Specialist with over 10 years experience. She provided guidance to the organization on issues based on health and child rights.
