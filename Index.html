WELCOME TO INCLUSIVE CARE

Empowering Every Voice.

We are a Ghanaian not-for-profit organisation that provides high quality technical advice, project management, research and implementation support services, using local talent and expertise to enable Ghana's social systems to reach greater heights.

Who We Are

Driving change through purposeful action and inclusive values.

Our Vision

An all-time available consultancy for community-based rehabilitation services and community development programs.

Our Mission

To advance inclusive development in Ghana, through advocacy, rehabilitation, education, and social consultations.

Core Values

  • Diversity
  • Integrity
  • Teamwork
  • Inclusiveness
  • Equity

Our Programs

Advocacy & Awareness

Leading advocacy efforts to advance policies and raise public awareness on disability and mental health inclusion.

Empowerment & Livelihood

Providing capacity building for marginalized people and their families to secure their rights and livelihood.

Rehabilitation & Audits

Offering direct rehabilitation and conducting public accessibility audits to ensure inclusive environments.
