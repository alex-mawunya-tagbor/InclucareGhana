Empowering Every Voice. Building Inclusive Futures.

The Inclusive Care Foundation is dedicated to combating social exclusion, inequality, and discrimination against marginalized groups in Ghana, including **persons with disabilities, the elderly, and children**. We utilize the Community-Based Rehabilitation (CBR) Matrix to advance inclusive development.

Our Foundation

Mission & Vision

**Vision:** An all-time available consultancy for community-based rehabilitation services and community development programs.

**Mission:** To advance inclusive development in Ghana, through advocacy, rehabilitation, education, and social/public consultations.

Core Values

Diversity Integrity Teamwork Inclusiveness Equity

Background

Established in **March 2025** and headquartered in **Peki, Volta Region, Ghana**, the Inclusive Care Foundation is a duly registered non-profit. Our primary aim is to be a voice for the oppressed and advocate for their rights, aligning our efforts with **Sustainable Development Goals** and utilizing the **Community-Based Rehabilitation (CBR) Matrix** as our framework.

Our Programs: The CBR Matrix in Action

Policy & State Agency Guidance

Organizing and advancing policy guidance to state and non-state agencies concerning the marginalized, especially persons with disabilities, children, and the aged.

Inclusion Advocacy & Education

Promoting disability and mental health inclusion through policy advocacy, public education, and comprehensive awareness creation.

Rights Capacitation

Capacitating people with disabilities, children, the aged, and their families to understand, demand, and secure their fundamental rights.

Rural Social Intervention

Developing and implementing targeted social intervention projects in vulnerable rural communities to address immediate needs.
