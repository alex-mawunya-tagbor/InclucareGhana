Empowering Every Voice. Building Inclusive Futures.

The Inclusive Care Foundation is dedicated to combating social exclusion, inequality, and discrimination against marginalized groups in Ghana, including **persons with disabilities, the elderly, and children**. We utilize the Community-Based Rehabilitation (CBR) Matrix as a framework to advance inclusive development.

Who We Are

Our Vision, Mission, and the Values that Drive Inclusive Development

Our Vision

An all-time available consultancy for community-based rehabilitation services and community development programs.

Our Mission

To advance inclusive development in Ghana, through advocacy, rehabilitation, education, and social/public consultations.

Our Core Values

Diversity; Integrity; Teamwork; Inclusiveness; Equity
